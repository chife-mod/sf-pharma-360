Color

Dark-first. Deep navy canvas, one cyan/teal primary family, and a small categorical accent set. Rule: at most four dominant accents per screen; one accent owns one entity (card / KPI).

Radius & Motion

Radii scale from 6px (chips) to fully round (pills, avatars). Motion is restrained: one standard easing, short durations, hover never brighter than active.

Standard easecubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)--v2-ease — every hover, accordion, layout transition
